Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite team members?
You can invite team members by navigating to the Team page and clicking the "Add Member" button. Enter their email address and select their role to send them an invitation link.
Can I connect external calendars?
Yes, Donezo integrates with Google Calendar and Outlook. Go to Settings > Integrations to connect your accounts.
How to upgrade my plan?
Navigate to Settings > Billing and click "Change Plan". You can choose between Pro, Team, and Enterprise tiers.
